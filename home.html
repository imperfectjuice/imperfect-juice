Business Idea

The issue of municipal solid waste (MSW) in Hong Kong is crucial. In 2022, it has recorded over 11,000 tonnes of MSW to the landfills daily, and food waste accounted for around 30% of the total, including ‘ugly food’.

Bless, a cold-pressed juice company established in Hong Kong since 2014, aiming to build a healthier world through juice. "Perfectly Imperfect Juice" creates with imperfect fruit or vegetables that sourced directly from Hong Kong local farmers.

Perfectly Imperfect Juice is using cost leadership, providing differentiation of product while obtaining a lower cost than competitors. Priced at HKD $20 per bottle.

Consumers could purchase from Bless’s online and retail store, Wellcome, and City’super.

SWOT Analysis

Strengths

  • Bless’s HPP technology for longer shelf life and quality.
  • Cost advantages among competitors.
  • Aligns with market demand for natural food.

Weaknesses

  • Unstable supply due to seasonality and climate.
  • Negative perception towards imperfect food.

Opportunities

  • Growing juice market at 4.39% CAGR.
  • Increasing health awareness post-pandemic.
  • Demand for sustainable products.

Threats

  • Easy imitation by competitors.
  • Market saturation leading to price competition.

Resources

To realize the "Perfectly Imperfect Juice" project, we will be transforming imperfect yet high quality fruits and vegetables into cold pressed juices.

Human Resources: Core team of five founders, phased hires.

Physical and Financial: Startup capital HK$500,000, equipment like cold press machine.

Stages: Startup, Growth, Maturity, Renewal.
